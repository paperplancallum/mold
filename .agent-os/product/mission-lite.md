# Product Mission (Lite)

MoldScope is a mobile-first AI-powered application that helps homeowners identify and assess mold growth from testing kit samples by providing instant analysis, severity ratings, and actionable health recommendations.

MoldScope serves homeowners who purchase DIY mold testing kits and need expert-level analysis without the cost and delay of traditional lab services. Unlike lab analysis that takes 7-14 days and costs $50-200, MoldScope provides instant AI-powered mold identification, health risk assessment, and remediation guidance in under 60 seconds, making professional-grade mold testing accessible to everyone.
